Supply Nation

What is Supply Nation?

Supply Nation stands as Australia's premier supplier diversity organisation, a driving force behind the economic prosperity of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander businesses. This entity serves as the bridge connecting Indigenous suppliers with potential buyers in both the public and private sectors, playing a pivotal role in fostering sustainable growth, innovation, and collaboration within the Indigenous business community.

Decor Systems is Supply Nation Certified

At Decor Systems, our commitment to Supply Nation is deeply rooted in our CEO's heritage. Owner David Faulkes is of Aboriginal descent, making us 51% Indigenous-owned.

But our support extends beyond heritage; it's ingrained in our business ethos. Our Contours of Country product range is a sustainable Indigenous partnership, with 15% of sales proceeds returned to the artist.

We've also actively contributed to First Nations projects, such as the RAWA Community School, where we donated products for their classrooms.

Your Reconciliation Action Plan

RAPs underline your commitment to fostering reconciliation between Indigenous and non-Indigenous Australians, providing a framework to contribute to closing the gap on Indigenous disadvantage, building meaningful relationships, and acknowledging the contributions of Indigenous peoples to our nation.

Our Supply Nation certification can help top achieve the goals and commitments outlined in your RAP. When you choose to partner with us, you actively engage with an Indigenous-owned and operated business, contributing to the economic and social aspects of reconciliation.

Indigenous Inclusion Mandates

For builders, architects, and subcontractors, partnering with Supply Nation is not just about procurement; it's about meeting Indigenous participation targets. Choosing us as a supplier can assist in meeting these targets effectively.
