GET TO KNOW US

If you're looking for a new role and you've landed here, then you're probably also interested in what it's like to work for Decor Systems. Here we share what to expect from working with us and a little about our culture.

WHAT TO EXPECT
HERE

Your Career

Our work has an impact on peoples’ lives and we offer career opportunities that will excite and challenge you more than you ever imagined. We want you to grow your career in a way that suits you, so you can make the best of your skills and thrive from day one.

Our Clients

We’re very proud to enjoy some of the highest client satisfaction rates in our sector and consequently retain our clients far longer than the industry average.

Your Development

Ours is a culture rich in continuous learning and opportunity. Whether it is professional qualifications, new legislation, new technology, new client accounts or a new role we firmly believe in helping support our employees to develop their skills and knowledge.

Fun

We work hard but we want you to enjoy being here and to thrive outside of work too. We have fun together while focusing on appreciation, recognition, wellness and philanthropy.

Your Wellbeing

We believe it is vital to balance the needs of the business with the needs of everyone who works here. Wellbeing here covers physical, emotional, financial and social wellbeing, and includes flexible working and family-friendly policies.

Innovation

We’re always looking for new creative ideas to make our clients’ lives easier. We strive to provide a stimulating environment in which your work will be enjoyable, challenging and often ground-breaking.

Technology

We invest in the latest technologies to match our ground-breaking business solutions. We use the most up -to-date technology and software to help staff and clients do the best work they can.

Work

Our focus is on high-quality, high-value work that makes a difference for our clients. This leads to engaging and fulfilling work for all of us.

WE CELEBRATE THE WINS

We incorporate small but impactful initiatives that make work that little bit more rewarding.

MONTHLY SPIN THE
WHEEL

Each person in our business works by the Decor System values and is nominated each month to spin the wheel when they demonstrate behaviours that align with them. Several prizes are available from a coffee to a house clean to a weekend away! It's how we reward high s performance.

OUTBOUND GAME

Our sales team participates in the 'Outbound' game where they nominate prizes upon reaching specific point milestones, earned through achieving sales initiatives and targets. This incentivised approach benefits all parties involved, resulting in a win-win scenario!

BIRTHDAY SURPRISES

We never miss a birthday! Whether it's doughnuts, flowers or pizza, we make sure we celebrate birthdays here at Decor Systems.

CELEBRATING MILESTONES

Work anniversaries are celebrated at Decor Systems. We have a list of gifts to choose from when you reach milestone anniversaries.

OUR COMMITMENT TO DIVERSITY, INCLUSION, AND EQUAL OPPORTUNITY

Join Us at Decor Systems: Commitment to Diversity, Inclusion, and Equal Opportunity

At Decor Systems, we foster an inclusive and diverse work environment where every team member is valued and respected. We believe that a diverse workforce drives innovation, creativity, and success. Our commitment to anti-discrimination and equal opportunity is at the core of our values and practices.

We proudly provide equal employment opportunities for all individuals, ensuring a fair and unbiased hiring process. Our workplace is free from discrimination and harassment, promoting a culture of respect, dignity, and inclusion. We celebrate the unique backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences each employee brings to our team.

What We Stand For:

  • Equal Opportunity: We ensure that all employment decisions are based on merit, qualifications, and performance. Discrimination of any kind is strictly prohibited.
  • Inclusive Environment:We actively promote diversity and inclusion within our workplace, recognising the value of different backgrounds and experiences.We actively promote diversity and inclusion within our workplace, recognising the value of different backgrounds and experiences.
  • Harassment-Free Workplace:We maintain a zero-tolerance policy towards sexual harassment and bullying. We encourage employees to report any incidents without fear of retaliation.
  • Reasonable Accommodations:We provide reasonable accommodations for qualified individuals with disabilities and pregnant employees to ensure they can effectively perform their duties.
  • Respect for Freedom of Association:We respect employees' rights to join or not join associations of their choosing, including labor unions, without fear of reprisal or harassment.

At Decor Systems, we are committed to creating a supportive, innovative, and respectful environment for all. Join us and be a part of a company that values your unique contributions and is dedicated to your growth and success.
