Sound Solutions: Enhancing Acoustics for Aged Care Comfort
APPLICATIONS

4 JUL 2024

Many building commissioners, designers, and care providers often underestimate the significant impact of noise, particularly on older individuals. By raising awareness about the causes and harmful effects of noise, we aim to provide solutions to minimize these effects through thoughtful design and management. This approach aims to promote optimal health and well-being among care facilities and private home residents. As Florence Nightingale aptly stated, "Unnecessary noise is the cruellest abuse of care which can be inflicted on either the sick or the well."
