Our meticulously crafted acoustic panels live at
the intersection of sculpture and sound. Acoustic panels
synchronise effortlessly with even the most ambitious
architectural vision.

Rich sensory design has been a Decor speciality for over
50 years. We’re here to help you create alluring spaces
with exceptional sound management.





DECOR 360

1. Design & Development


We provide complimentary shop drawings, top-notch
project management and access to our R&D department
where you can access prototypes and expert advice on
installation.



2. Value Engineering


We understand that the design vision doesn’t always
marry up with the budget. We can value-engineer any
concept to achieve similar design outcomes within the
specified budget.



3. Samples


We have a selection of samples on hand and ready to
despatch. But we also offer a custom sample service so
that you can see exactly how your specification will look.
Order online or give us a call.



4. Compliance


We're all about rigorous product testing and
certification in sustainability, fire safety, and acoustics.
We've got an all-star line-up of partnerships with
specialists in these fields. If there's a certification you're
after and we don't have it, we'll do our best to make it
happen!



5. Local On-Site Support


The sum of the parts is greater than the whole rings
true at Decor Systems. With combined experience more
than 100 years, the Decor Systems team are the best in
the business.



6. Execution & Delivery


We support you right through until installation and
beyond by providing site-specific delivery options and a
high degree of prefabrication. Our Customer Care team
ensure the delivery process is seamless and your panels
come sealed and protected so they arrive in the best
condition.



7. Exceeding expectations at speed


We pride ourselves on customer service and aim to
exceed expectations on every project and at every stage.



8. Celebrate & Review


At Decor Systems, we're all about raising a glass to
our achievements. After every project, we take the time
to review, analyse, and savour the moment. Cheers to
success!
